ABOUT

Keep your personal data safe and secure

LogBook allows you to harness the power of technology to eliminate the need for cumbersome paper logs and complex spreadsheets. Now you can even use your web browser to collect notes and even files such as Images, Pdf's, Videos, Cad files, Excel sheets etc. to sync directly into the LogBook, which is Encrypted i.e. totally secure and inaccessible to anyone else without your permission.

Build stronger communication and Collaboration

LogBook gives you the platform to keep your team in the loop on what’s going on in your project. Clearer communication ensures a more efficient operation.
